PunchWatch Privacy Statement

Last updated: April 21, 2026

Hey there, Boxer!

I'm George Yameen, the creator of PunchWatch. I built this app because I love boxing and wanted a better way to track my punches. Here's the straight talk on how your data is handled.

What PunchWatch Does & Doesn't Do

PunchWatch uses your Apple Watch sensors to track your punches and provide feedback. Your punch data stays on your device. We collect anonymous usage analytics to improve the app.

Your Data Stays Put

PunchWatch processes your punch tracking locally on your Apple Watch and iPhone. Your punch stats, performance metrics, and training data stay on your devices. Anonymous usage data (screens viewed, button clicks, app interactions) is sent to analytics services to help us improve the app.

Analytics We Use

We use Google Analytics and Smartlook to understand how the app is used. These services collect: device type, iOS version, which screens you view, and how you interact with the app. No personal information or boxing performance data is collected. You can opt-out of analytics in the app Settings.

Kids Can Play Too

PunchWatch is safe for users of all ages. We don't collect any personal information that identifies users.

Changes to This Statement
